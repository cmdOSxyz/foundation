# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to building an open, respectful and collaborative community around cmdOS.

Everyone should feel welcome regardless of experience, background or identity.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful
- Providing constructive feedback
- Sharing knowledge
- Collaborating professionally
- Focusing on technical discussion
- Respecting different viewpoints

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling
- Publishing private information
- Malicious contributions
- Spam

---

## Project Values

cmdOS values:

- Transparency
- Security
- Determinism
- Collaboration
- Technical excellence
- Documentation-first development

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary restriction
- Permanent removal from the project

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers privately.

---

Thank you for helping make cmdOS a welcoming and professional open-source community.
