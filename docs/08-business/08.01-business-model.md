# 08.01 Business Model

## Overview

The Business Model defines how cmdOS creates, delivers, and captures economic value through its AI Execution Operating System ecosystem.

cmdOS is designed as a platform business rather than a traditional software product.

The core business model is based on:

- AI execution services
- Platform subscriptions
- Developer ecosystem
- Enterprise solutions
- Marketplace economy

Core principle:

↓

AI Execution Platform

↓

Agents + Capabilities

↓

Value Creation

↓

Revenue Generation
