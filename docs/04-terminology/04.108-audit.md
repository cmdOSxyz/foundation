# 1. Purpose

This document defines the canonical meaning of **Audit** within cmdOS.

Audit is the canonical historical accountability mechanism responsible for recording, tracking, reviewing, and analyzing activities performed by Users, Agents, Systems, Tools, and Processes.

Audit enables cmdOS to maintain transparency, security, accountability, and operational understanding across the entire AI Execution Operating System.

Every significant operation within cmdOS shall generate Audit information.

---

# 2. Definition

An **Audit** is a structured historical record of an activity, operation, decision, state change, or interaction performed within cmdOS.

Audit records:

- who performed an operation
- what operation occurred
- when it happened
- why it happened
- what changed
- what result was produced

Audit provides operational accountability.

---
