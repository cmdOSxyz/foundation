# Identity

Version: 1.0  
Status: Canonical  
Category: Foundation

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the canonical meaning of **Identity** within cmdOS.

Identity is the canonical representation of an entity's existence, ownership, authority, and accountability within the AI Execution Operating System.

Identity enables cmdOS to determine:

- who or what exists
- who or what is acting
- what authority is available
- what actions are permitted
- who is responsible

Every Object within cmdOS shall possess an Identity.

---

# 2. Definition

An **Identity** is a unique and verifiable representation of an Object, Actor, Service, or System participating within cmdOS.

Identity establishes:

- uniqueness
- ownership
- authority
- trust
- accountability
- relationships

Identity answers:

"Who or what is performing this operation?"

---

# 6. Identity Characteristics

Every Identity shall be:

- unique
- verifiable
- persistent
- traceable
- governable
- auditable
- accountable

Every Identity shall contain:

- Identity Type

- Credentials

# 19. Identity Security Principles

Identity follows these principles.

### Unique

Every Identity is distinguishable.

---

### Verifiable

Identity ownership can be proven.

---

### Least Privilege

Identity receives only required authority.

---

### Traceable

Actions remain attributable.

---

### Revocable

Compromised identities can be disabled.

# 20. Identity Invariants

The following statements shall always remain true.

1. Every participating entity has an Identity.
2. Every Identity has a unique identifier.
3. Identity ownership is explicit.
4. Identity verification is governed.
5. Identity actions are traceable.
6. Identity permissions are controlled.
7. Identity lifecycle is observable.
8. Identity cannot bypass security governance.

These invariants apply throughout the cmdOS platform.

# 22. Summary

Identity defines the existence and accountability of every participant within cmdOS.

It provides the foundation for authentication, authorization, security, delegation, trust, and governance across humans, Agents, services, devices, and external systems.

By separating Identity from Authentication, Authorization, and Credentials, cmdOS establishes a secure foundation for autonomous AI execution while maintaining transparency, control, and accountability.

Identity is therefore the canonical participation primitive of the AI Execution Operating System.
