# 1. Purpose

This document defines the canonical meaning of **Policy** within cmdOS.

Policy is the canonical rule definition mechanism responsible for describing expected behavior, restrictions, requirements, and operational standards within the Governance framework of the AI Execution Operating System.

Policies allow cmdOS to control how Users, Agents, Tools, Systems, and Processes operate.

Every Governance system within cmdOS shall contain one or more Policies.

---

# 2. Definition

A **Policy** is a formal rule definition that describes required, allowed, restricted, or prohibited behavior for Entities operating within cmdOS.

Policy defines:

- what is allowed
- what is prohibited
- what conditions apply
- who is affected
- when enforcement occurs
- what happens when violated

Policy converts Governance principles into enforceable rules.

---
